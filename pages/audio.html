what is bibliothèque?

bibliothèque is a personally curated publically available collection of literature across the web. Come here to find e-books and audiobooks that I find culturally relevant, important, or just cool. Click on one of the above buttons to begin exploring bibliothèque.

bibliothèque works on the stolen and unceded lands of the Sḵwx̱wú7mesh, Tsleil-waututh, and xʷməθkʷəy̓əm peoples. We are commited to the sovereignty of all indigenous peoples on their lands. No copyrighted materials are hosted on this site.
