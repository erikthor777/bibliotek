welcome to bibliothèque!

what is bibliothèque?

bibliothèque is a personally curated publicly available collection of literature across the web. Come here to find e-books and audiobooks that I find culturally relevant, important, or just cool. Think of this as my own personal library, accessible to you. Click on one of the above buttons to begin exploring bibliothèque.

why?

The web is filled with an unimaginable amount of information. Sometimes it can be hard to navigate. Sometimes it can be hard to find the good stuff. Sometimes it's overwhelming and you don't know where to start. That's how I feel all the time on the web, so I decided to make a place where I keep my favourite literature.

bibliothèque works on the stolen and unceded lands of the Sḵwx̱wú7mesh, Tsleil-waututh, and xʷməθkʷəy̓əm peoples. bibliothèque is committed to the sovereignty of all indigenous peoples on their lands. No copyrighted materials are hosted on this site. Background image from Pinterest.
